My basic sell rules:

Phase 1: Before 1R, exit on a close below the 21EMA low

Phase 2: After 1R, exit on a close below entry

Trim: Trim 33% at 3R

Final TP: After 3R, exit the rest on a close below the 21EMA low

Risk: 21EMA low at entry

All based on the daily chart.



Hybrid RS
Date is pulled from the yfinance and FMP APIs. The subscription only costs a few dozen dollars right now, so it’s not an issue, but I’m open to switching if there’s a better API out there.

For Hybrid RS, I’m combining RS (3 timeframes) + Fundamental RS + Industry RS. Current weighting is 5 (1:2:2) : 2 : 3. Still testing.

Once I get something I’m reasonably confident in, I plan to restart posting my watchlist.
I pick up tickers that appear in multiple of those 7 lists.

Momentum 97
Volatility Contraction Score
21EMA Watch
4% Gainers
Relative Strength 21 > 63
Vol Up Gainers
High Est. EPS Growth

Specifically, I post stocks that appear three times or more across those lists as “duplicate tickers in the watchlists.”

The reason is that tickers from 4% Gainers and Vol Up Gainers often overlap.

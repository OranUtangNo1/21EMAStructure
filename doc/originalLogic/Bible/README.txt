# Bible Commentary
The documents in this section are articles and social media posts by the creators of the strategies we aim to replicate in this application, which have been compiled and organized.

## Scan Section
This section compiles information regarding ticker scans.
Please note that it is not certain whether the scans described here are currently in use.

## TradingDecision Section
This section compiles information regarding trading decisions.

## SocialMedia Section
This tier collects the owners’ posts on social media.
While strict definitions of the scans are not provided, this information may be useful for understanding the evolution of the owners’ strategies and scans over time.

## other Section
This tier collects information not included in the other tiers.

# Important Notes
The documents do not necessarily fall within the scope of this application’s reproduction.
While some content pertains to strategies we wish to reproduce, others likely no longer in use are also included.

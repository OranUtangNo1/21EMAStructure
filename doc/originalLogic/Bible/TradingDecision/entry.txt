 - Structure Pivot (LL-HL / HH-LH)
This indicator scans across all specified lengths at the same time and detects both bullish (LL→HL) and bearish (HH→LH) structures within them. 
From the setups found, it automatically selects the pivot line based on the chosen mode—Tightest Structure, Longest, or Shortest. This allows the indicator to adapt its breakout/breakdown level depending on whether a tighter entry (Tightest Structure), a larger structure (Longest), or a quick short-term pullback (Shortest) is preferred.

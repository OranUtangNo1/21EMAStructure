

My 9 scan setups

・21EMA
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Weekly % 0 to 15%, DCR% >20%, 21EMA at ATR -0.5R to +1R, 50SMA at ATR 0R to 3R, PP Count 30d >1, Trend Base

・4% bullish
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Rel Vol >1x, Daily % >4%, From Open % >0%, RS 1M >60

・Vol Up
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Rel Vol >1.5x, Daily % >0%

・Momentum 97
Avg Vol 50d >1M, 1W % change top 3% percentile ≥ 0.97, 3M % change top 15% percentile ≥ 0.85, Trend Base

・97 Club
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Hybrid RS >90, RS 1M >97, Trend Base

・VCS
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, VCS 60 to 100, RS 1M >60

・Pocket Pivot
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Price >50SMA, green candle, volume higher than the highest volume in the past 10 days.

・PP Count
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, PP Count 30d >3, Trend Base

・Weekly 20% plus gainers
Market Cap >1B, Avg Vol 50d >1M, Weekly % >20%

Common factors: ADR% 3.5 to 10, ex Healthcare
DCR% = Daily Closing Range %
Trend Base = Price >50SMA, 10WMA >30WMA
Hybrid RS = a composite score of industry strength, fundamentals, and multi timeframe RS
VCS = proprietary Volatility Contraction Score
PP Count = Number of Pocket Pivot occurrences
I mainly pull data from yfinance and FMP(starter plan), with the Nasdaq API as a backup.
I added two new fields to the scan results: earnings within the next 7 days and PP Count (30d).

PP Count (30d) scans how many times a Pocket Pivot has occurred in the past 30 sessions.

For Pocket Pivot, the full definition usually includes more conditions, but I keep it simple: price above the 50SMA, a green candle, and volume higher than the highest volume in the past 10 days.
